# research/assets/

Per-symbol deep-dive notes, one markdown file per universe member named
after the symbol with the `-USD` suffix dropped (e.g. `BTC.md`, `ETH.md`).

The Saturday daily routine and the every-4-hours per-asset rotation routine
both write here. Recommended sections:

- **Thesis** — one paragraph on why this symbol is in the universe.
- **Last 30/60/365 day price action** — short narrative.
- **Volatility regime** — current realised vol, percentile vs own history.
- **Correlation with BTC** — current 30d corr, whether it is decoupling.
- **Sector context** — its role in {L1, payments, oracle, layer2}.
- **Risks** — concrete and current, not generic.
- **Watch points** — levels or signals the next reader should know.

Refresh cadence: each symbol gets touched at least every 5 days through the
combination of Saturday's deep-dive (3 symbols) and the 4-hourly rotation
(2 symbols per fire * 6 fires/day = 12 touches/day across 10 symbols).
